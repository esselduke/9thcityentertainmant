Plain-language explanation of cookies and tracking.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How Cookies Work:

When you visit our website, we may place cookies on your device. These cookies store small amounts of data, such as:

  • Your preferences and settings
  • Whether you're logged in (if applicable)
  • Pages you've visited
  • Your general location (based on IP address)
  • How you found our site

First-Party vs. Third-Party Cookies:

  • First-party cookies: Set by 9TH CITY ENTERTAINMENT and used only by our website
  • Third-party cookies: Set by other companies (like Google, YouTube, Spotify) when their content is embedded on our site

Session vs. Persistent Cookies:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

How to control cookie usage.

You have several options for managing cookies:

Option 1: Use Our Cookie Manager (Recommended)

Scroll to the top of this page to manage your cookie preferences using our interactive cookie manager. You can enable or disable specific cookie categories and save your preferences.

Option 2: Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session
  • Get notified before a cookie is set

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Option 3: Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics: Browser Add-on
  • Network Advertising Initiative: Opt-out Tool
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: Consumer Choice Page

Note: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly without cookies enabled.
