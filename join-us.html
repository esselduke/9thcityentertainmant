HOW WE REVIEW APPLICATIONS

All submissions are reviewed by our team within 10 business days. If your profile matches our needs, we'll reach out to schedule an interview or creative review. Due to high volume, we may not be able to respond to every application personally.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you charge fees for applications or auditions?

No. 9TH CITY will never request payment, gift cards, or fees in exchange for reviewing applications, offering contracts, or providing opportunities. Any such request is fraudulent.

How do I report a scam?

If you receive suspicious contact claiming to represent 9TH CITY, report it immediately to report@9thcityent.com.

What types of roles do you offer?

We hire across A&R, production, marketing, design, operations, and business development. Roles range from full-time positions to project-based contracts and internships.
