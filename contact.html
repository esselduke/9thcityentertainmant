FAQS

Where is 9TH CITY ENTERTAINMENT located?

9TH CITY operates with teams in Accra and Toronto, working across music, production, and brand partnerships with a global reach through digital platforms.

How can I apply for internships or employment?

For information on career opportunities, visit our Join Us page for available positions and submission details.

How can I learn more about artists signed to 9TH CITY?

For the latest artist news, releases, and profiles, explore our Roster page or follow our verified social platforms for real-time updates and content.

How do I submit my demo or get signed?

9TH CITY does not accept unsolicited demos. Artist submissions are accepted by referral through verified managers, producers, or official partners only.

I'm a company or brand looking to partner with 9TH CITY. Where should I direct my inquiry?

For partnership and brand collaboration opportunities, contact our Partnerships team at partnerships@9thcityent.com.

How can I license 9TH CITY music for film, TV, or advertising?

All licensing and sync requests should be directed to sync@9thcityent.com with full project details, usage type, and contact information.

I believe I may be owed royalties. Who can I contact?

For royalty or publishing inquiries, contact our Rights & Royalties team at royalties@9thcityent.com. Please include your full name, contact information, and a brief description of your query.

I found 9TH CITY music being used or distributed illegally. How can I report this?

If you notice unauthorized use or distribution of 9TH CITY content, report it to report@9thcityent.com.

I'm a member of the media. How can I reach your communications team?

For interviews, press materials, or media requests, contact press@9thcityent.com.

I'm an investor or potential partner. Who do I contact?

For investment or strategic collaboration inquiries, please email invest@9thcityent.com.
